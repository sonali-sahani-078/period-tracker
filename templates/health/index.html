Period Care

Know your cycle better

Healthy cycle basics

A typical cycle is around 21 to 35 days, and bleeding often lasts 2 to 7 days.

Tracking start date, duration, and flow helps detect changes early.

Daily period support

Hydration, sleep, light activity, and iron-rich meals can reduce fatigue and cramps.

Use heat packs, stretching, and regular meal timing to support mood stability.

When to seek care

Consult a clinician for severe pain, very heavy bleeding, or sudden major cycle changes.

Also seek medical advice if cycles are frequently missed or occur too closely together.
